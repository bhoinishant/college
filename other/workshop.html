SHORT TERM COURSES CONDUCTED

Short term on Cadence VLSI design tools : 25-29 September 2018

The five days short term coursewas organized for students and teachers to train them on analog and digital IC design using this EDA tool. The design schematic, simulation, physical design and synthesis were explained for analog as well as for digital ICs and hands on practice on these tools were facilitated to the participants.

Short term course on Cadence OrCAD PCB Design Tool : 15-17 October 2018

The workshop was organized for students and teachers in order to train them for designing the electronic circuits simulate them for their functionality and further design the PCB. This results in generation of Gerber and drill files which may be used to fabricate PCB using CNC machine.

TEQIP III sponsored 5 days Workshop for Students on PCB design, Fabrication and Circuit development : 15- 19 January 2019

A five days TEQIP III sponsored workshop for students was organized during 15-19 January 2019. The workshop covered training on various PCB and circuit design software like LT Spice, Eagle, Proteus and OrCAD. The circuit simulation and further PCB design was explained to the students and later rigorous hands on practice was facilitated.Participants practiced manual as well as automated PCB fabrication. Students of various engineering departments of Engineering College Bikaner, College of Engineering and Technology (CET), and Govt Polytechnic College participated in the workshop.

TEQIP III Sponsored 5 Days FDP for Faculty Members and Students on Design and Fabrication of Electronic Circuits Using CAD Tools : 2-6 September 2019

A five days faculty development program on automated Design and Fabrication of Electronic Circuits using CAD Tools was organized in Electronic Instrumentation and Control Engineering Department for creating awareness of latest industrial requirements skills in design and fabrication of electronic automation products among students as well as teachers. More than hundred teachers and student participants from various colleges attended and benefitted with this quality improvement program. The latest computer aided design tools were explained and a complete practical knowledge for automated designing & fabrication of PCB, placement of electronic components, development and testing of electronic circuits was imparted during five days lab based training. All the participants were given a task of design, fabrication, circuit development and testing for five identified projects.

Two day Workshop for PCB Design, Fabrication and Circuit Development for Polytechnic students : 13-14 March 2020

As per the request received from department of Electrical Engineering and Department of Electronics Engineering of Government Polytechnic College Bikaner, a two day special workshop was organized by EICE department on 13 and 14 March 2020. The workshop covered PCB design and fabrication using Eagle and OrCAD Softwares and Eleven Lab CNC PCB fabrication machine. Further students developed complete circuits by mounting, soldering and testing of the circuits.
